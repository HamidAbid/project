Course Description

Developing a solid foundational knowledge of food safety is essential. If you are new to the food industry and involved in manufacturing or supporting food production, you need to understand your role in protecting consumer health. It is important to you and essential to your business. This course provides that foundational background.
This training will allow you to know the elements of a HACCP system and the Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR).

Our HACCP I training introduces you to the fundamentals of food safety while maximizing your learning for effective practical application.

  • Identify food safety risks and understand the importance of preventive controls and prerequisite programs
  • Learn the 7 principles of HACCP , essential for effective food safety management
  • Understanding your role in protecting consumer health and maintaining food safety in your organization
  • Know the requirements of the RSAC and how to set up a preventive control plan (PCP)
  • Familiarize yourself with the necessary documentary requirements


Dive into interactive learning: Our live virtual classes are led by passionate instructors, offering hands-on training designed specifically for the food industry. Enjoy a rewarding experience that promotes sharing experiences and teamwork, while consolidating your learning with a final exam.

What you'll need: A computer with a camera and a high-speed internet connection. For the best experience, we recommend a headset with a microphone. Also, make sure you're using an up-to-date web browser like Firefox, Chrome, Safari, or IE Edge.

Immediate certification: At the end of the training, receive a certificate confirming your new skills.


Professionals and technical personnel from the agri-food sector or related fields wishing to familiarize themselves with the basic concepts of the HACCP system.
