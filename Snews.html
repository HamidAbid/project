FDA and FSMA Rules for the Food Industry

The FDA requires registration of food sites to track the source of contamination or a potential bioterrorism threat.

Since 2011, the FSMA law has strengthened these registration rules. Sites must now provide more information to the FDA and renew their registration every two years.

Key points for FDA renewal:

  • Without renewal, the registration will expire on December 31, 2024
  • Registration or renewal is free
  • Online renewal via your FDA Industry Systems account (except exceptions)
  • An FDA recognized Unique Identifier (UFI) is required (currently DUNS number is accepted)
