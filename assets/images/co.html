About

Leader in food safety for the agri-food industry since 2005

Our mission

Our mission is to be the leader in our industry and to make food safety accessible to businesses. Innovaltech stands out for its practical expertise in the factory and a personalized approach in the development and implementation of quality systems (HACCP/GFSI) and its recognized training program.

Our vision

Our strategy must serve the world's major challenges. To meet these challenges, we develop innovative and sustainable solutions with the aim of improving food safety in the industry. We firmly believe that every consumer has the right to healthy and wholesome products.


The founders

 

Pierre Loiselle President

Co-founder of Innovaltech, Pierre Loiselle has an impressive practical experience in the agri-food sector. Working in the food sector since 1989, he has become an expert in his field. Food safety and preventive maintenance of production equipment no longer hold any secrets for him! He now makes his knowledge available to agri-food companies.

 

Valérie Charest Vice-president - B.Sc. Microbiologist, Mcb.A.,RMCCM

A professional microbiologist since 1996, Valérie Charest is a true specialist in food safety, quality system development and implementation (HACCP, GFSI) and process validation. Co-founder of Innovaltech, she has extensive expertise in microbiology and practical work experience in factories. Today, she assists North American agri-food companies in food safety.
