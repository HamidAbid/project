Who Are We?

Created in 2005 by Pierre Loiselle and Valérie Charest, MJA Food safety was born from a shared vision: to offer cutting-edge food safety services for the agri-food industry.

Our culture is deeply rooted in safety and quality. Every member of our team is committed to meeting the highest standards, including those established by HACCP and GFSI.

Through our continued commitment to innovation, MJA Food safety has become a leader in its field. We are proud of what we have accomplished and continue to emphasize a culture of safety and quality in everything we do.
